Company Profile
Security Systems Integrator Since 1995

IPM Technovation Company Limited

IPM Technovation Company Limited specializes in the design, installation, and integration of complete security systems. With more than 30 years of experience, IPM applies high-quality technology, devices, and software to create secure, modern, connected, and scalable systems for future enterprise growth.

Integrated Smart Security Solution

IPM provides intelligent security systems for large organizations, covering CCTV & VMS, AI Video Analytics, Access Control, Face Recognition, License Plate Recognition, Visitor Management, Video Wall, Drone Integration, Network Monitoring, and Backup Power.

All systems can be designed to connect into a centralized control center, helping security teams monitor, verify, alert, and respond to incidents efficiently.

AI Operation Center (AIOC)

IPM develops the AI Operation Center (AIOC) concept to elevate centralized incident and security management by connecting data from multiple systems into one platform, including CCTV Analytics, Face Recognition, LPR, Access Control, Drone, IoT, E-map, Dashboard, and Report.

AIOC supports a structured workflow from Detect, Verify, Alert, Respond, and Report to enable real-time decision support and more efficient operations.

Network Security & Infrastructure

Beyond physical security systems, IPM also focuses on secure network and infrastructure foundations, covering Network Design, VLAN, Firewall Policy, VPN, Server, Storage, Network Monitoring, Log Management, Backup, and Recovery.

This ensures CCTV, Access Control, Face Recognition, LPR, Command Center, and AIOC can operate securely, reliably, and continuously.

Technology Platforms

IPM selects high-standard technologies and platforms such as Milestone XProtect VMS, FaceMe Security, AI Video Analytics, CCTV System, Access Control System, License Plate Recognition, Command Center & Dashboard, and Network Security Infrastructure.

IPM uses open-platform technologies to support flexible integration, future expansion, and practical deployment for each organization.
